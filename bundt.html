Bundt Cake Recipe

Dark chocolate Bundt cake recipe

  1. First preheat your oven to 350 F and grease a 12-inch Bundt cake pan, butter or oil works fine. You also want to gather your ingredients ensuring your milk, eggs, sour cream, and butter are at room temperature.

  2. Then in a large bowl mix together butter and sugar until the mixture becomes fluffy. This can be done easily with an electric mixer at medium speed. In another bowl mix together milk and sour cream until lumps go away also add vanilla extract.

  3. Then beat in the eggs to your butter-sugar mixture 1 at a time. Once it is combined add the sour cream and milk mixture and stir until it is all incorporated. Then you can add the salt and baking soda to this mixture.

  4. Now slowly add in your flour and mix until it is just barely combined, then do the same with the cocoa powder. You shouldn't over-mix here or your cake will be doughy. you should also gently stir in the melted dark chocolate you don't need to completely combine it.

  5. Once there are no more lumps in your batter you should pour it into your cake pan and smooth the top. Then place it in the middle rack of the oven and set a timer for about 40 minutes. Check with a toothpick after the timer is up and leave for another 5 min if it still comes out wet.

  6. After removing it from the oven let it cool completely and then sprinkle on your powdered sugar. It can be stored in the fridge for up to a week but it tastes best fresh.

Ingredients Amount
Unsalted Butter 1 cup or 2 sticks (room temp)  
Cane Sugar 2 cups  
Eggs 2 large (room temp)  
All-purpouse flour 2 cups  
Cocoa powder 1/3 cup  
Vanilla extract 1 tsp  
Sour cream 2/3 cup  
Whole milk 1.5 cups  
Baking soda 1 tsp  
Salt 1/4 tsp  
Powdered sugar 3-4 tbsps  
Dark choclate 2 oz. (melted)
